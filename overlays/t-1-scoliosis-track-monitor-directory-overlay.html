Scoliosis Track Monitor 是一个在米兰理工大学 NECST Laboratory 举办的 Hackathon —— "Creativity, Science and Innovation: Ideate, Invent, Innovate" 中开发的原型项目。该系统通过三点式传感器测量背部角度，实时计算 Cobb 角，并通过蓝牙传输至手机 App，实现可视化追踪与历史数据分析。项目面向青少年脊柱侧弯的早期筛查，强调低成本、无辐射、家庭可用，提供了一种技术介入健康管理的全新方式。
Scoliosis Track Monitor is a prototype developed during the Hackathon "Creativity, Science and Innovation: Ideate, Invent, Innovate" at the NECST Laboratory, Politecnico di Milano. Using a three-point sensor system to calculate Cobb angles, it transmits data via Bluetooth to a mobile app for real-time monitoring and historical analysis. Targeted at early scoliosis screening in adolescents, it offers a low-cost, radiation-free, family-friendly solution for proactive spinal health management.
