本报告通过五个练习，系统探讨了数据处理中的关键统计方法，并将其应用于气候、人口、健康、农业和空间几何等真实场景中。在完成这些任务的过程中，我不仅加深了对数据可视化和建模技术的理解，还提升了处理复杂数据集和评估模型有效性的能力。
我主要负责练习四中有关脊柱侧弯分析的部分。在这一部分，我使用多元线性回归（MLR）模型对青少年临床数据进行了建模，探索影响 Cobb 角度的关键因素。通过变量筛选与残差分析，我发现年龄、Risser 骨龄评分和治疗方式在预测脊柱弯曲程度中具有显著作用。这一过程让我掌握了基本的预测建模流程，并增强了我在医学数据分析方面的实战能力。
总的来说，本课程帮助我建立了系统的统计思维方式，使我能够在不同类型的数据中提取有意义的洞见，并以更加严谨和批判性的方式对结果进行评估。
This report systematically explores key statistical methods in data processing through five exercises, applying them to real-world contexts such as climate, demography, health, agriculture, and spatial geometry. Through these tasks, I deepened my understanding of data visualization and modeling techniques, while improving my ability to handle complex datasets and evaluate model performance.
I was primarily responsible for the scoliosis analysis in Exercise 4. In this section, I applied a Multiple Linear Regression (MLR) model to adolescent clinical data to explore key factors influencing the Cobb angle. Through variable selection and residual analysis, I identified age, Risser score, and treatment type as significant predictors of spinal curvature. This process gave me hands-on experience in predictive modeling and strengthened my practical skills in analyzing medical datasets.
Overall, this course helped me develop a structured statistical mindset, enabling me to extract meaningful insights from different types of data and evaluate results in a more rigorous and critical manner.
